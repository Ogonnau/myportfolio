Wixsin Sales Dashboard

This Excel interactive Dashboard utilizes a Revenue Dataset encompassing data from five Canadian states, spanning January to June 2015. The data were presented as different bits of data that were further consolidated into one data using various excel tools. The dataset was provided by Quantum Analytics NG.

Key Insights:
- A line chart showcased the sales trend, indicating that March and April saw the highest revenue, while January recorded the lowest.
- A bar chart illustrated the top-selling products by revenue, with Maximus UM-43 leading and Maximus UC-41 trailing.
- A pie chart identified Ontario as the top state by revenue.
- A donut chart demonstrated the revenue contribution by category, with Urban contributing the most and Youth the least.

Recommendations
Focus on promoting sales during the peak months of March and April. Look into boosting sales of Maximus UC-41 and directing more marketing efforts towards Ontario, Alberta, and British Columbia, which are the top revenue-generating states. Additionally, prioritize products in the Urban category for further promotion, while exploring ways to increase revenue from the Youth category.

Sales Analysis for Godswill Enterprise

The dataset focuses on Goodwill Enterprise and underwent cleaning and analysis using Excel tools. The objective was to develop a dashboard illustrating profit trends from 2011 to 2016. This dashboard showcases daily, monthly, and yearly profits, identifies peak and off-peak seasons for profits and highlights the top 10 best-selling products.

One key recommendation stemming from the analysis is for the company to explore the reasons behind lower sales profits of other products. Strategies should then be formulated to enhance the profitability of these products, potentially leveraging insights gained from top-selling products to inform these strategies.
